Our Mission

We educate leaders who make a difference in the world.
Each element of the School's mission—to educate leaders who make a difference in the world—is infused with meaning.


"Leaders" When we talk about leaders, we mean people who embody a certain type of competence and character—both the competence that comes from the general manager's perspective the School cultivates and the character to understand the difference between being self-interested and self-centered. "Make a Difference" Making a difference means people who create real value for society, and who create value before claiming value. In the World it involves embracing the view that the world desperately needs more leaders to address its most urgent and challenging problems, and that virtually none of these problems can be addressed without business leaders playing a vital role.
